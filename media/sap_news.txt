SAP announced that the jobs at SAP Landing Page for refugees from Ukraine is live. To support refugees from Ukraine, SAP is rolling out a dedicated onboarding process for refugees who have arrived in Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Germany, Hungary, Poland, Romania and Slovakia. This includes buddy support with an existing Ukrainian employee, mental health support and dedicated learning and language courses, childcare options (in selected countries) and advanced payment options for new hires. SAP is also working to extend this to other countries.
